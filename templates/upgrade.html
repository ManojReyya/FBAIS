Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be charged the prorated difference.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and popular digital payment methods including UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Is there a free trial for Premium?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for the Premium plan. No credit card required to start. If you don't upgrade, your trial period ends automatically with no charges.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is always yours. If you cancel, you can export all your data in multiple formats. We retain your account for 30 days in case you want to re-subscribe.
How many team members can I add?
Free plan allows 1 user. Premium plan includes up to 3 team members. Enterprise plans have unlimited team members with role-based access control.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes! If you're not satisfied with your purchase within the first 7 days, we offer a full refund. No questions asked. Contact support@fbais.com for assistance.
